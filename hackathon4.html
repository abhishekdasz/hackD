About HINT Hackathon:-

Hack in the North is the largest student run hackathon in india organized by IIITA Since the inception of HINT in 2016, we have experienced remarkable growth with the increasing participation and enthusiasm of Problem solvers and Developers all over the country.Our mission is to empower students from different backgrounds and encourage them to dream big and build a project from scratch.
